Purbasha COVID10 Update !!!

Due to concerns surrounding COVID-19 (Coronavirus) and in accordance with the Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), Purbasha executive committee has decided to cancel Bangautsav, scheduled for May 9, Saturday in DeSana Middle School. We are very disappointed that we are unable to hold this event for our members and supporters.
The Purbasha EC Team and the BOD are closely accessing and following the CDC , Federal and State Government orders based on the Progress of the Situation and taking into consideration any possibility of changes in Schedule /Cancellation of Future Events
Please take care of yourself and family in these days of unprecedented national calamity. Follow guidelines as prescribed by the CDC , State and Federal Government .
